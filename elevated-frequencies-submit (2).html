Entrepreneur and Chicago Music Nexus co-founder Olivia Mancuso cues up insider tips and real-world stories to help aspiring artists turn their passion into profit. Each week, she shares personal insights alongside interviews with artists, promoters, label owners, agents, and more.

What Olivia Looks For
  • Original tracks only, no uncleared samples
  • Music with genuine underground energy
  • Tracks that tell a story or create a feeling
  • Lesser-known artists who deserve more ears
  • Completed, production-ready tracks
  • Artists engaged in their community
